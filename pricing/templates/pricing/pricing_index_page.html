Beginner Youth Class

Introduce your kids to the basics and fundamentals of Tang Soo Do. A perfect starter class for any kids to start their journey or to give it a try!

Youth Class

Students in this class build off the foundation of basics learned in the Beginner Youth Class and hone more difficult techniques. Students begin to learn more intermediate-advanced level forms. This class also includes introductions to fighting, weapons, breaking, ground fighting, and more.

Adult Class

This is a class for students ages 14 and up and will cover all ranges of Tang Soo Do training in a progressive manner.

FIMAP

A traditional Filipino Indonesian Martial Arts Program emphasizing weapon based fighting with sticks, knives, bladed weapons, and various improvised weapons, as well as "open handed" techniques without weapons.
